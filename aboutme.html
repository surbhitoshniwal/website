Hello there! A little more about me..

I am Surbhi Toshniwal - a young enthusiastic Mumbai-dweller, seeking to be inspired and hoping to have an impactful living.

Professionally a Chartered Accountant, currently working as a Brand Entrepreneur. "But how? why? Explain this a little bit" is usually the question I face when I tell anyone this. So lets address that first - Wanted to become a Chartered Accountant since the longest time (8th Std to be precise - I know, I saved up on career counselor fees :P). Post that, like most 20 something year old, confused - I began exploring in other areas. I have always been dazzled by the world of brands and business. My interest in brands led me to my current role! and I'm lovin it!

I love the process of creation and bringing ideas to life. Culturally curious. Lifetime Student. Bharatanatyam Graduate. Wannabe fancy tea drinker. And always looking for people to play table tennis with.

Oh. and I absolutely love travel. So much to learn, so much to observe and so much to imbibe! Till now, I have travelled over 14 countries around the world. Many more to go..
